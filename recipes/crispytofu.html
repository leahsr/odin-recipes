Crispy Tofu with baked potatoes

Description

You must try this amazing dish even if you are not a fan of Tofu. You can serve this dish with every side dish you want, but I enjoy it most with baked potatoes and some kind of vegteables like broccoli.

Ingredients

Tofu

  • 1 smoked Tofu
  • 1 Cup of oat milk
  • 1 Cup cornflakes
  • 1 Cup flour
  • 2 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon paprika pulver
  • 1 Tablespoon garlic pulver
  • 1 Tablespoon pepper

Potatoes

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Spices

Steps

Potatoes

  1. Slice up the potatoes
  2. Mix the potatoe slices together with the oil and your favourite spices
  3. Through them in the oven at 200°C

Tofu

  1. Cut the Tofu in slices
  2. Mix the oat milk, the soy sauce and the spices together in a bowl
  3. Crush the cornflakes in a bowl
  4. Dip the Tofu in the milk, then in the flour, than in the milk and after that into the cornflakes
  5. Fry the Tofu sticks in a pan until they get a golden colour
