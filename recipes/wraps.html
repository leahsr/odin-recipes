Refreshing Wraps with roasted chickpeas

Description

This wraps are perfect for dinner parties with your friends because everbody can add their favourite Ingredients! Furthermore these wraps are refreshing and still savory.

A perfect side dish for these are potatoe wedges!

Ingredients

  • Wraps
  • Chickpeas
  • Sour Cream
  • Salad
  • Cucumber
  • Paprika
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Chop all the vegteables into small pices
  2. Mix the sour cream with salt, pepper, garlich and paprika
  3. Fry the chickpeas in a pan with some oil and add your favourite spices
  4. Heat up the wraps in the oven or microwave but pay attention that they stay soft
