Gorgonzola Gnoccis

Description

You need to try these creamy Gnoccis. They are easy to make and super delicous!

Ingredients

  • 1 package gnoccis
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cups cream
  • 200ggorgonzola
  • olive oil
  • spices

Steps

  1. Fry the gnoccis in the olive oil until they are crispy brown
  2. Put the gnoccis aside and fry the onions in a new pan
  3. Add the cream and the gorgonzola cheese and let it melt
  4. Season the sauce as you like and add the gnoccis
