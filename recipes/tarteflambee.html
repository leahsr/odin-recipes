Tarte Flambée with zuccini and onions

Description

This is crispy Tarte Flambée is super delicious, easy to make and veggie.

Ingredients

  • 1 package tarte flambée dough
  • 1 zucchini
  • 2 onions
  • 1 cup crème fraîche
  • 1 package grated cheese
  • spices

Steps

  1. Role out the tarte flambée dough
  2. Mix the crème fraîche with your favourite spices
  3. Spread the crème fraîche on the dough
  4. Slice up the onions and the zuccini and put them on top
  5. Put some cheese on it
  6. Put it in the oven and bake it according to the dough package

In total: 2€ per Portion
